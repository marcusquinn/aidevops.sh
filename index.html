Get started in seconds...

1

Open the Terminal app on your computer

2

Copy/paste: bash <(curl -fsSL https://aidevops.sh/install) Enter

3

When setup, type opencode Enter. Then type /models Enter, Ctrl-A, and setup your OpenCode Zen or Anthropic access. Choose Claude Opus 4.5. Ctrl-F to Favourite models. Enter, Esc, Esc.

Now, just ask OpenCode in your terminal what aidevops does, and how to use it.

And, for a smarter experience, try Tabby and Zed.

AI DevOps Features & Capabilities

AI DevOps (aka: aidevops, aiops, ai ops) is a toolkit for your AI assistants to have better knowledge of all your digital infrastructure and assets, to help with creating, managing, coding, and solving errors.

Multi-Service APIs

Hosting, Git, security, monitoring, and deployment all in one place.
